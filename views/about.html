About
Autum Watch Company was born out of a passion for time and craftsmanship. Founded in 2016 by watch enthusiast Elias Reinhardt, Autum began with a simple vision — to create elegant, durable, and intelligent timepieces that blend timeless style with modern functionality. Inspired by the quiet strength of autumn, our watches reflect precision, purpose, and personality in every tick.
What Sets Us Apart
At Autum Watch, we believe time is more than a measure — it’s a memory in the making. Every watch we create is a blend of timeless elegance and modern craftsmanship, designed to accompany you through life’s most meaningful moments.

With a deep respect for tradition and an eye for innovation, we craft each piece from the finest materials, ensuring that every detail tells a story of dedication and artistry.

Autum Watch is more than a timepiece. It’s a companion for your journey — a celebration of where you’ve been and where you’re going.
